Saito is a Paradigm Shift in Blockchain

Saito is a foundational advance in blockchain. It fixes the underlying economics of the network so that honest nodes produce blocks for free but attackers are forced to burn money to do the same.

Solving this puzzle requires answering fundamental questions that other blockchains cannot what is the value of work to the network, and how can we pay market prices for it? The strength of the solution is the way its elegantly solves "foundational" problems considered unsolvable in other networks.
